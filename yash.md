## Phase 7: The "Pro-Level" Intelligence Hub (Massive Content Expansion)

## Goal
Transform the app into a comprehensive global discovery engine covering Pop Culture, Business, and Tech.

## Tasks
1. **Multi-Source Scraping:** - **Entertainment:** Use the `TMDb API` for trending Movies/Actors and `Spotify Web API` for top Songs/Singers.
   - **Business:** Scrape `Yahoo Finance` or `Google News` for trending Companies and Trade updates.
   - **Autos:** Scrape `Car and Driver` or `Top Gear` for viral car news.
2. **"Perfect Image" Engine:** - Primary: Wikipedia `originalimage`.
   - Secondary: Use `Unsplash API` or `Bing Search API` to fetch a high-res background if Wikipedia is empty.
3. **Infinite Content Expansion:** Create sub-pages for:
   - 🎵 **Music & Singers** (Live charts)
   - 🎬 **Movies & Actors** (Box office & viral trailers)
   - 🏎️ **Automotive** (New launches & tech)
   - 🏢 **Corporate & Startups** (Acquisitions & stock moves)
4. **Professional UI Refinement:**
   - Use **Inter** font with tight tracking for a "Bloomberg" look.
   - Add a "Live Ticker" at the top showing real-time trending keywords.
   - Add a "Depth" effect: subtle 3D shadows and hover-glows on cards.
